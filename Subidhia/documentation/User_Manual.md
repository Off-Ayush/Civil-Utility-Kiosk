## Introduction

## User Login

1. Enter your **Consumer ID** or **Mobile Number**
2. Enter your **Password** or **OTP**
3. Tap **Login Securely**

> For OTP login, tap "Request OTP instead" to receive a one-time password on your registered mobile.

## Dashboard Features

### View Current Bill
- Your current bill amount is displayed prominently
- See the due date and consumption details
- Tap **Pay Now** for quick payment

## Making Payments

### Payment Methods
1. **UPI** - Scan QR or enter UPI ID
2. **Card** - Debit/Credit card payment
3. **Net Banking** - Bank transfer
4. **Wallet** - Digital wallets

### Payment Process
1. Select payment method
2. Enter payment details
3. Confirm the amount
4. Complete verification
5. Download receipt

---

## Registering Complaints

### Complaint Types

**Electricity:**
- Power Outage
- Billing Issue
- Meter Fault

**Gas:**
- Gas Leak
- Low Pressure
- Billing Issue

**Water:**
- Water Supply Issue
- Water Quality
- Billing Issue

**Waste:**
- Missed Collection
- Illegal Dumping

### Filing a Complaint
1. Select complaint type
2. Describe the issue in detail
3. Upload supporting documents/photos (optional)
4. Submit and note the Tracking ID

### Tracking Status
Use your Tracking ID to check complaint status:
- **Registered** - Complaint received
- **In Progress** - Being addressed
- **Resolved** - Issue fixed
- **Closed** - Completed

---

## New Connection Application

1. Select connection type (Residential/Commercial/Industrial)
2. Fill in applicant details
3. Provide address information
4. Upload required documents:
   - ID Proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Voter ID)
   - Address Proof
5. Submit application
6. Note your Application Number
